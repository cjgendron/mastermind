Welcome to Mastermind!

Directions:

A pattern is hiding behind the five white squares. To win this game, you must guess the pattern.

  1. Pick a color in each column.
  2. Click "Submit."
  3. Results will appear to the right of your guess.
    • A red square means that one of your guesses is the right color in the right spot. (It will not show you which guess is correct; it will only show how many guesses were correct.)
    • A white square means that you have the correct color, but in the wrong spot. (Like the red squares, it will not specify which guess earned the white square.)
  4. Continue to guess until you win. OR... After 12 incorrect guesses, the game is over because you are a giant loser.
Notes:
  1. There are three hints available. Use them wisely.
  2. Colors may repeat (once or more) in the pattern.
  3. Suggestion - Try five different colors for your first guess.
